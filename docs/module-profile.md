**MIDI Control** — A [Blish HUD](https://blishhud.com/) module that lets you play Guild Wars 2 instruments with a real MIDI keyboard or controller.

**Features**
- 🎹 **MIDI-to-keyboard mapping** — Play any supported GW2 instrument directly from your MIDI device
- 🗂️ **Custom JSON keymaps** — Drop `.json` files into the `midi-keymaps` directory to add your own instrument mappings alongside built-ins
- 🔄 **Auto octave swap** — Automatically shifts octaves (`9`/`0`) when you play outside the current range, with configurable delay for multi-octave jumps
- 🔁 **Auto-reconnect** — Detects disconnects and retries every 10 seconds until your device returns
- 🎛️ **Live keymap preview** — See note-to-key mappings and a real-time "Recent Sends" log in the settings panel
- 🔇 **Toggle keybind** — Global hotkey to instantly mute/unmute note sending
- 🛡️ **Focus guard** — Optionally blocks keypresses when GW2 is not the active window

**Supported Instruments**
- General (Manual — for any instrument without a dedicated keymap)
- Grand Piano
- Bass Guitar (Auto)
- Choir Bell
- Flute (C & E)
- Harp (Auto)
- Horn (C Auto & E Auto)
- Lute (Auto)
- The Minstrel (Auto & Manual)
- Verdarach (Auto)
